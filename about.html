      ABOUT US

      Welcome to Ankita Computer & Stationers (ACS) – your trusted source for premium-quality housekeeping supplies and stationery essentials. Established in 2007, ACS has grown to become a leading supplier to corporate clients across Bangalore, Chennai, and Mumbai, committed to supporting businesses and households with products that enhance cleanliness, organization, and productivity.

      Our leadership team is comprised of experienced professionals:

      • Mr. Magesh Harji Patel (Founder & CEO) – Bringing over 20 years of expertise in corporate supplies, including stationery, housekeeping, and pantry items, across major Indian cities.
      • Mr. Mahesh Patel (Operations Head) – With over 15 years of extensive experience in office consumables, computer stationery, and related fields, he oversees operations in Mumbai and Bangalore.

      Managed by a team of professionals with a combined experience of over 40 years, ACS operates with a focus on providing swift and reliable service. Our central location allows us to cater efficiently to a broad client base.

      Why Choose Us?

      As a dedicated supplier, we build long-term partnerships by delivering dependable, high-quality products at competitive prices. We understand the unique needs of our clients and offer personalized recommendations and support to ensure you have exactly what you need, when you need it.

      Thank you for choosing Ankita Computer & Stationers as your supplier. We look forward to supporting your efforts to maintain clean, organized, and productive spaces.

      Our Mission

      Our mission is to make it easier for clients to create well-organized, clean spaces by delivering high-quality supplies with exceptional service. We believe that the right supplies can enhance daily operations, making a positive impact whether in the office, school, or home.

      Our Values

      • Quality Products: Carefully selected, durable products that offer excellent value.
      • Sustainability: Committed to providing eco-friendly solutions to reduce environmental impact.
      • Customer Satisfaction: Personalized service and support, tailored to your unique needs.

      Our Offerings

      • Housekeeping Supplies: Comprehensive range of cleaning products, tools, and equipment for maintaining safe and welcoming spaces.
      • Stationery Essentials: Extensive selection of office supplies, educational tools, and customized products for businesses, schools, and individuals.
